We're a family-run coffee house passionate about great coffee and great food. We source high-quality beans from around the world and offer everything from a classic espresso to CBD and alcoholic coffees. Our kitchen serves freshly made paninis, baguettes, and jacket potatoes, and our cake display is always stocked with local bakes and our famous cookie pie. Vegan? No problem — we have plant milks, vegan cheese, and vegan cakes available.
